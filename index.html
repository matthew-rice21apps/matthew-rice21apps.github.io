Final Project

Hello! Welcome to my Final Project page. You will find the project that i made for CSC 106 at the University of Rhode Island. My project is a coloring book program that is a lot of fun. When you are ready to play the game click the button that says start. Once Clicked you will see a page that gives instructions. Read the instructions and when you are ready click the button that says next. After you click next, you will see seven levels to chose from. Click on a level that you want and once clicked it will take you to the picture of the level you clicked on. When you are on the level, click on a color and press down on the mouse and drag your mouse where you want the color to go. Color in the pictures with any color you want and have fun!
